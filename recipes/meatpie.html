Meatpie Sauce


Description

Meat pie is a savory dish consisting of a pastry crust filled with a mixture of seasoned meat and other ingredients. It's a popular dish in various forms around the world, with different cultures adding their own twists and flavors to the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh parsley
  • ¾ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 ½ teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 ½ teaspoons pepper sauce (such as Frank's Red Hot®)
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Finely chop the parsley, garlic, and oregano. You can do this by hand with a sharp knife or use a food processor for quicker results. If you prefer a smoother texture, pulse the ingredients in the food processor until finely minced.
  2. Transfer the chopped parsley, garlic, and oregano to a mixing bowl.
  3. Add the olive oil, red wine vinegar, and red pepper flakes to the bowl.
  4. Season the mixture with salt and black pepper to taste. Start with a small amount of salt and pepper and adjust according to your preference.
  5. Stir the ingredients together until well combined. Taste the chimichurri and adjust the seasoning if needed, adding more salt, pepper, or red pepper flakes according to your taste.
  6. Let the chimichurri sauce sit at room temperature for at least 15-30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. Serve the chimichurri sauce alongside grilled meats such as steak, chicken, or turkey. It can also be used as a marinade or dipping sauce for bread.
  8. Store any leftover chimichurri sauce in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week. Allow it to come to room temperature before serving again, as the olive oil may solidify when chilled.

Enjoy your homemade Meatpie Sauce........
